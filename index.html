FAQ

What services do you provide?

+
We provide complete construction and interior design services including residential construction, commercial building, interior design, modular kitchen, false ceiling, renovation, civil contracting, labour supply, and home loan assistance.

How long does construction take?

+
Project timelines vary based on scope. Residential projects typically take 6-12 months, interior work 2-4 months, and renovation 1-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and ensure on-time delivery.

What areas do you serve?

+
We are based in Ashiyana, Lucknow and primarily serve Lucknow and surrounding areas. We take on projects across Lucknow including Gomti Nagar, Indira Nagar, Aliganj, and nearby regions.

Do you provide home loan assistance?

+
Yes! We provide complete home loan consultation and assistance including documentation support, bank coordination, and application processing to help you secure the best loan rates for your construction project.

What is your pricing structure?

+
We offer competitive pricing based on project scope, materials, and requirements. We provide detailed quotations after site visit and consultation. Our transparent pricing ensures no hidden costs and best value for your investment.
